# Utility Behind $Hush token

The **$HUSH token** is the core utility asset of the HushSense ecosystem. It is designed to be the economic engine, aligning incentives between the community data contributors, who set up the monitors, and the commercial entities, who use the data to ensure compliance and plan for healthier urban spaces.

$HUSH powers every key interaction across the network, enabling scalable and decentralized growth for environmental monitoring across Africa.
