# Why DLT? Why Hedera?

### Why Hedera Hashgraph? The Real-Time Advantage

While traditional blockchains like Bitcoin or Ethereum provide security, their design prioritizes decentralization over speed and cost, making them slow and expensive which is a disaster for a real-time, high-volume sensor network.

**HushSense is built on Hedera Hashgraph** because its unique architecture delivers the triple threat necessary for a scalable DePIN: Speed, Low Cost, and Energy Efficiency.

| Feature                        | Hedera's Advantage                                                                                                                                                        | Why It Matters for HushSense                                                                                                                                                                             |
| ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Speed (High Throughput         | Hedera uses a unique Hashgraph consensus (Asynchronous Byzantine Fault Tolerance - aBFT) that processes thousands of transactions per second (TPS).                       | We need to handle thousands of simultaneous sound measurements _per minute_ from users across a city. Instant data finality means the map is truly real-time.                                            |
| Predictable, Low Fees          | Hedera guarantees extremely low, stable transaction costs (a fraction of a cent per transaction), regardless of network congestion.                                       | Since we are running microtransactions for every noise report, fluctuating or high fees would make the entire incentive model economically unfeasible. Low fees ensure sustainable operations.           |
| Energy Efficiency              | Hedera's consensus mechanism does not rely on power-hungry "mining" (Proof-of-Work). It is one of the greenest DLTs available.                                            | As an environmental project focused on public health, we must maintain a minimal carbon footprint. Our solution should not contribute to another environmental problem.                                  |
| Hedera Consensus Service (HCS) | This service provides a decentralized, auditable log specifically designed for IoT devices and enterprise data streams, offering verifiable timestamps and fair ordering. | HCS allows us to rapidly timestamp and order the high volume of noise reports coming from different phones, ensuring the data's integrity and sequence are trustworthy when presented to city officials. |

#### 2. Automating Incentives and Ownership (The Motivation Problem)

If people aren't paid or recognized, they won't collect data. Blockchain enables a self-sustaining economy:

* Automatic Rewards: Smart contracts automatically manage the token rewards (HushSense Coins) and NFT distribution, ensuring that contributors are paid instantly and fairly based on the validity and novelty of the data they provide.
* **Decentralized Ownership:** By giving users tokens and NFTs, we turn them into owners and stakeholders in the network's success. They are no longer just customers; they are active infrastructure providers.

### Why Decentralized Infrastructure (DePIN) and Why Hedera ?

Noise pollution is a problem of trust and incentives. Cities need trustworthy, accurate data to create policy, but they can't afford to deploy millions of sensors. Citizens have the devices (smartphones), but they need a reason to contribute their data consistently and a guarantee that their contribution matters.
